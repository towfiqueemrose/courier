About Us

The demand and potential of the online courier and the sector are growing. At the same time, the need and acceptance of online-based courier services are also increasing. So here comes the Eagle Express, one of the biggest logistic companies in Bangladesh to meet the increasing demand. 

Eagle Express is known as the first logistic marketplace in Bangladesh. We were established in 2019. We are mainly a cargo and freight company.  We deliver in almost all the districts and Upazilas in Bangladesh, so it can be said that we are giving delivery to the whole country. We are the fastest delivery service provider in Bangladesh. 

The Features of Eagle Express Online Courier:

  • Tracking facility of parcels in online

  • COD charge only 1% 

  • There is no return charge

  • Product Collection from your address (Now available for Dhaka,Chittagong,Sylhet,Narayanganj,Savar and Gazipur.)

  • Delivery to 64 district cities and 492 Upazilas, etc. 

The Charge list for delivery is given below:

  • Next-day Delivery Cost

    • The next-day delivery cost is only 100 taka.

  • Express Delivery Cost

    • The express-day delivery cost is only 70 taka in between 48 hours.

Some of the famous brands that put their trust in us are - Rokomari.com, Jenneys, Lafz, Raw Fashion, Gadget & Gear, and hoping many more brands will take our services very soon.
