Local Courier Service

Local Courier Service means the shipping of parcels and important documents inside the country. It is one of the most challenging services. Because the parcel can be broken or lost anytime, anywhere. To deliver the parcel of a customer safely is the biggest challenge for a courier service provider.  

The courier services provide different kinds of facilities like - Same day delivery, within next day delivery, delivery within 4 days, etc. The same-day delivery is applicable for the urgent products. The next day delivery is generally good for normal delivery. And in the express delivery, the product will reach the customers within 4 days maximum.  

Difference Between Parcel and Courier Services

There are a few differences between parcel and courier services. The parcel services are better for individual and light goods. On the Other hand, courier services are best for bulk and heavy items.  

Another difference is the speed of delivery. Courier services can be a better option for same-day and next day deliveries, while parcel services are better for standard deliveries. So, if it is needed to send large goods to transport quickly, a courier service is ideal. For lighter parcels, it will be cheaper to go with a parcel delivery service.
