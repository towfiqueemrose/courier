DT Service and Express

Eagle Express, the largest courier service in Bangladesh has come to serve valuable customers with different kinds of exclusive services. We have Cash on delivery service, Home delivery service, Local service, Online parcel delivery service, E-commerce courier service, and many more advanced services. Moreover, through Eagle Express, you can track your parcels online easily. Some of our services are described below -

Home Delivery Service

In the past, after ordering a product, the parcel was sent to the nearby courier services branches. The customer had to collect the parcel from the courier services branch. But now the situation has been changed. Many courier services are delivering the parcels directly to the customer’s home and this service is known as ‘Home Delivery Service’. Eagle Express also provides a Home delivery service for the customers. In this process, a customer can order and deliver his product staying at home.

Cash on Delivery or COD System

Now customers can receive their products by staying at home. Also, they can pay after receiving their parcels and checking them properly. The method is known as Cash on Delivery or COD System. There are some advantages of the COD system, such as - the advantage of verifying the ordered products after delivery, paying after receiving the products properly, no fear of money lost because you are paying after getting your products, and many more facilities. That’s why the COD system is a good process of paying for your parcels. Eagle Express’s COD system is a trusted method of payment for the customers. Through the COD system, the customers get the facility for payment with full security after receiving the product without any advance cost.

Local Courier Service

Delivering a parcel or documents inside the country is known as Local Courier Service. Generally, local courier services deliver the parcels inside the country. The parcels need to be sent very carefully because they may be lost or broke unwillingly. That why this service needs more safety. The biggest challenge for a courier service provider is to deliver a parcel safely. The courier services provide different kinds of delivery facilities such as - Next day delivery, Same-day delivery for urgent parcels, delivery within 3 to 5 days, etc. The next day delivery is enough for general delivery. The same-day delivery is applicable for delivering urgent products. In the express delivery service, the product is delivered to the customers within 3 to 5 days maximum.

Online Parcel Delivery Service

Eagle Express has been set up online courier services across the capital city to serve e-commerce or online shopping customers in the fastest time. In addition to the well-known courier services that provide services across the country, many courier services have been launched for delivery only in Dhaka. Eagle Express Courier Service is working in the categories of Delivery on the same day, Delivery on the next day, Express delivery, Emergency Delivery, etc. to deliver the goods-documents, parcels safely to the desired address in a short time, in compliance with the rules and regulations.

Express Delivery Services

Eagle Express has an exclusive ‘Express Delivery Service’ facility, which has been made it unique from the other courier services. In this service, You can send any parcel to any place in the country. The parcel will be delivered within 4 to 5 working days. The parcel will be collected from your address. The Features of Express Delivery Service are given below -
  • Delivery in maximum 4-5 days across the country,
  • Product Collection from your address directly,
  • Service facilities available 7 days a week,
  • Delivery to 64 district cities and 492 Upazilas of the country,
  • In the case of non-delivery within 4-5 days, a regular delivery charge (Taka 75) will be applicable.
