How to Activate Instant Payment

Instant Payment system allows you to receive your delivery payments in real-time. Follow the simple steps below to activate this feature:

Step 1: Login to Your Account

Visit our website and login to your merchant account using your credentials.

Step 2: Go to Payment Settings

Navigate to the Payment Settings section from your dashboard menu.

Step 3: Select Instant Payment

Choose the "Instant Payment" option and click on "Activate Now".

Step 4: Provide Bank/Mobile Account Details

Enter your bank account or mobile financial service (bKash, Nagad, etc.) details where you want to receive payments.

Step 5: Confirm & Activate

Review your information and confirm to activate Instant Payment. Once activated, you will receive payments in real-time after each delivery.

Benefits of Instant Payment:

  • Real-time payment transfer after delivery
  • No more waiting for weekly or monthly settlements
  • Better cash flow management for your business
  • Transparent transaction tracking
  • Automated payment system
